
# Common Design Guidelines for AI Skills Framework Presentation

## Theme & Color Palette
- **Background**: Linear gradient from slate-900 to slate-700 (top to bottom) on all slides
- **Primary Text**: White (high contrast)
- **Role-Based Accent Colors**:
  - Software Developers: Blue (#3B82F6)
  - Operations & DevOps: Green (#10B981)
  - Product Managers/Business: Orange (#F59E0B)
  - Architects/Leadership: Purple (#A855F7)
  - Security: Red (#EF4444)
  - Data Engineers: Cyan (#06B6D4)

## Typography Standards
- Slide Titles: text-6xl or text-7xl, font-bold, white
- Section Headers: text-5xl or text-4xl, font-bold
- Sub-headers: text-3xl or text-2xl, font-semibold
- Body Text: text-2xl or text-xl, normal weight
- Captions: text-xl or text-base, opacity-80

## Layout Principles
- All slides: 1920px Ã— 1080px
- Consistent header (60-80px height), body (variable, grows), footer (40-50px height)
- Maintain 40-60px padding on all sides
- Use component backgrounds (slate-800/slate-700 with opacity) over gradient for content blocks
- High contrast for all text
- Center content horizontally on every slide

## Component Styling
- Cards/Panels: Rounded corners (rounded-lg or rounded-xl), subtle shadow, semi-transparent dark background
- Icons: Use lucide-react, size-8 to size-12, inline with text or centered above
- Lists: Consistent bullet styling, adequate line-height (1.6-1.8)
- Tables/Matrices: Alternating row colors for readability, bold headers

## Universal Font
- Use only Inter or Arial throughout
